My Mission

As an Architects, I Supervised the design and construction of a 1,200-unit 2-bedroom resettlement housing scheme in Jibi-Abuja, showcasing expertise in large-scale residential projects. As Chief Architect, played a pivotal role in rehabilitating the National Mosque, collaborating with a team of 30 professionals, demonstrating leadership in complex architectural endeavors. Sole female representative on the National Mosque rehabilitation team, contributing diverse perspectives. Managed the 12-year construction of the Directorate of Road Traffic Services' head office, navigating challenges from government policy inconsistencies.

Gained expertise in architectural design and environmental sustainability in the government sector. Active in property construction, management, and rehabilitation, emphasizing interior spaces, solid waste management, and environmentally friendly technologies.
